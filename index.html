Neck Deep
Discography

Welsh pop punk Neck Deep was formed in 2012 as the D.I.Y. project of singer Ben Barlow and guitarist Lloyd Roberts. Part of the wave of pop-punk revival acts, Neck Deep cited influences like Fall Out Boy, New Found Glory, and Sum 41, offering an updated version of the genre's classic sound.
